## 1. App Overview

Chat Money Manager is a personal finance tracking app. It helps users record income, expenses, accounts, categories, budgets, recurring transactions, reminders, monthly summaries, and related financial notes.

The app is designed to work locally first. Most financial records are stored on your device so that basic money tracking can work even without an internet connection.

Some optional features, such as Google Sign-In, AI-assisted transaction parsing, cloud processing, and Google Drive backup, may send selected data off your device.

## 3. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, Chat Money Manager may collect or process the following types of information.

### 3.1 Account and Sign-In Information

If you choose to sign in with Google, the app may access or process:

* Email address
* Display name
* Profile photo URL
* Email verification status
* Authentication provider information

Google Sign-In is used for identity, cloud-related features, AI features, device session handling, and backup-related access where applicable.

The app does not intentionally copy your email address, display name, or profile photo into the local finance database.

### 3.2 Financial Data

When you use the finance tracking features, the app may store:

* Transactions
* Income and expense amounts
* Account names and account types
* Category names and category types
* Budgets
* Recurring transaction rules
* Reminder events
* Monthly summaries
* Notes attached to transactions
* Raw chat text used to create a transaction
* Soft-deleted records retained for history and backup consistency

This information is used to provide personal finance tracking, dashboards, calendar views, reports, budgets, reminders, backup, and AI-assisted suggestions.

### 3.3 AI and Chat Data

When you use chat-assisted transaction entry, the text you enter may be sent to backend services to create a suggested transaction.

The AI system may process:

* Your chat text
* Currency
* Timezone
* Locale
* Date information
* Account names and IDs
* Category names and IDs
* Suggested amount, type, category, account, date, note, and confidence score

AI suggestions are not automatically saved as final financial records. You must review, edit if needed, and confirm before a transaction is saved.

The backend does not intentionally store full AI prompts or full AI model responses. It may store sanitized metadata such as request status, confidence, matched account/category IDs, warning codes, model name, and timestamps for audit, safety, and debugging.

### 3.4 Backup Data

The app may allow manual backup export/import and Google Drive backup.

Backup files may include:

* Profile settings
* Accounts
* Categories
* Transactions
* Budgets
* Monthly summaries
* Recurring rules
* Reminder events
* Notes
* Raw transaction chat text
* Soft-deleted records

Backup files do not intentionally include Firebase authentication credentials, OAuth tokens, or API keys.

Manual backup files are selected, shared, stored, or deleted by the user. Google Drive backups, when enabled and authorized by the user, are stored in the user’s Google Drive app data storage.

Backup files are checksum-protected for integrity, but are not encrypted by the app itself.

### 3.5 Device and Technical Data

The app may process technical information such as:

* App-generated device ID
* Device platform
* Device model or hostname where available
* App version
* Firebase installation or App Check signals
* Request IDs
* Backend request status
* Error type
* AI usage counts or cost-related technical counters

This data is used for authentication support, device session handling, abuse protection, debugging, cloud service reliability, and operational monitoring.

### 3.6 Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may use local notification features to show reminders. Notification content may include reminder titles or related reminder text.

### 3.7 Purchases and Ads

The app may show ads with google admob and introduce in app subscriptions

## 5. Where Data Is Stored

### 5.1 On Your Device

The app stores most finance data locally on your device using a local database.

Local data may remain on the device until you delete it, clear app data, uninstall the app, restore from another backup, or use an available wipe/delete option.

### 5.2 Firebase and Google Cloud

When cloud-related features are used, some data may be processed or stored through Firebase or Google Cloud services, including:

* Firebase Authentication
* Cloud Firestore
* Cloud Functions
* Firebase App Check / Play Integrity
* Google Cloud Logging
* Vertex AI / Gemini for AI-assisted parsing

Cloud-stored data may include identity-related profile metadata, device metadata, AI audit metadata, AI review metadata, usage counters, and operational logs.

### 5.3 Google Drive

If you enable or authorize Google Drive backup, the app may store backup files in your Google Drive app data storage. These backup files may contain your local finance data.

### 5.4 User-Selected Export Locations

When you manually export or share a backup file, you choose where it is sent or stored. This may include local storage, email, Google Drive, or another app installed on your device. Once you share or export the file, that destination’s privacy practices may apply.

## 6. Sharing of Data

We may share or process data with service providers only as needed to operate app features, such as:

* Google Firebase for authentication, cloud functions, Firestore, App Check, and backend services
* Google Cloud / Vertex AI for AI-assisted transaction parsing
* Google Drive for backup storage when authorized by the user
* Android system services for local notifications and file sharing

We do not sell personal or sensitive user data.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards to protect user data.

Security practices include:

* Firebase Authentication for signed-in cloud features
* HTTPS/TLS for communication with Firebase, Google Cloud, and backend services
* Firestore security rules that restrict user data access
* Backend-controlled writes for sensitive cloud operations
* App Check / Play Integrity for abuse protection where configured
* Sanitized backend logging to avoid intentionally storing raw prompts, notes, tokens, passwords, full model responses, or full financial payloads

However, no system is completely secure. Local data, manual backup files, and Google Drive backup JSON files are not encrypted by Chat Money Manager at the application level. Users should protect their device and backup destinations.

## 8. Data Retention

Data is retained as follows:

* Local finance records remain on your device until deleted, cleared, replaced by restore, or removed by uninstall/app data clearing.
* Deleted transactions, budgets, recurring rules, and some other records may be soft-deleted, meaning they are marked as deleted but retained for history, consistency, and backup behavior.
* Manual backup files are controlled by the user.
* Google Drive backup files remain in the user’s Google Drive app data storage until replaced or deleted.
* Backend AI logs, usage records, device records, and operational logs may be retained for security, debugging, abuse prevention, and service reliability.
* Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or operational reasons.
